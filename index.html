One platform, two experiences

Built around the human tension in aging-in-place care.

Stars want dignity and independence. Guardians want enough signal to act with confidence. Aurora is designed to make both true at the same time.

Stars

Care that feels like support, not surveillance.

Aurora gives older adults a simple way to check in, see what is changing, and bring trusted people into the loop only where it matters.

  • Quick daily confidence

    Face capture and Apple Health trends help Stars understand their own baseline.

  • Less friction, more agency

    Simple screens keep health context, appointments, and reminders reachable.

  • Trusted help on standby

    Guardians can be invited into care without making every moment feel monitored.

Guardians

Enough context to know when to lean in.

Aurora organizes Star status, vitals, alerts, safe-zone events, medication changes, labs, and appointments into an action-oriented Guardian view.

  • Signal without constant checking

    Normal, stale, and alert states make it easy to scan what changed.

  • Faster response moments

    Alerts keep context close so Guardians can decide who to call and what to check.

  • A shared record of care

    Medication, lab, appointment, and location workflows reduce scattered follow-up.

Star experience

Designed for confidence at the point of use.

Star screens prioritize large targets, clear status, plain language, and a path from "how am I doing?" to "who needs to know?"

Guardian experience

A care cockpit for the people who respond.

Guardians do not need another dashboard to babysit. They need a fast read on whether their Star is normal, stale, or in need of attention, plus the workflows that turn concern into action.

Trust by design

Healthcare UX has to earn its place in the home.

Consent-led sharing

Care context is designed around explicit Star permissions and role-specific Guardian access.

On-device biometric boundary

Face enrollment is treated as a local security and capture-assurance layer, not a marketing gimmick.

Apple Health first

Aurora uses Apple Health as the wearable ingestion path for iOS, keeping integrations focused.

PHI-aware operations

Product, backend, and audit workflows are organized around protected health information boundaries.
